About

I am a freelance illustrator who loves green-haired girls and melon bread. I was born and raised in Osaka and graduated from the Department of Architecture Design.
I came to this world because I was invited to draw background illustrations for doujin games, but I soon switched to illustrating girls. I was lucky enough to be approached by a publisher, and I experienced short-term serialization in a 4-panel manga magazine, 4-panel game anthologies, contributions to illustrations for game setting original art collections, illustrations for children's books, and attempted character design for iOS apps.
I left the scene for a while due to various circumstances, but resumed my activities around 2023.

I also had a period when I operated various servers using FreeBSD and Debian as a hobby, and for some reason I have experience building a full-text search site for logs of a certain thread on 2ch using the full-text search system Namazu. I also have experience managing DNS for my own domain on a home server.
Maybe it's because I'm a completely humanities person, but I have a strong admiration and respect for the science field.

For Freelance work inquires please contact the Artist at umsisi.07@gmail.com

緑髪の女の子とメロンパンが大好きな兼業イラストレーターです。大阪生まれ大阪育ちの建築デザイン学科卒。
同人ゲームの背景原画に誘われてこの世界に来ましたがすぐに女の子イラストに転向。運良く出版社の方に声をかけていただき、4コマ漫画雑誌での短期連載/4コマゲームアンソロジー/ゲーム設定原画集イラスト寄稿/児童書の挿絵/iOSアプリのキャラデザイン未遂などを経験。
諸般の事情で一時界隈から離れていましたが2023年頃から活動を再開しました。

他に趣味でFreeBSDやDebianを使った各種サーバーの運用をしてた時期があり、2chのとあるスレッドのログ全文検索サイトを全文検索システムNamazuで構築した経験が何故かあります。自宅サーバーでの独自ドメインDNS管理経験もあり。
完全なる文系人間だからかもしれませんが理系分野への強い憧れとリスペクトがあります。
